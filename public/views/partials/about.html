Who

My early adult years were spent mostly behind a drum set, a bar counter and an apron. Succeeding in the music and service industry taught me how to collaborate with others, work quickly and efficiently and communicate well. Older and wiser, my desire to grow as a person lead me to Prime Digital Academy. Now I spend most of my time behind a computer writing code, designing and problem solving.
